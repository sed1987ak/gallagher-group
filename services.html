Service 02 · Featured Project

02

African AI
Tour Guide App

● Coming Soon

One of our flagship projects is an AI-powered tour guide application designed for travellers across Africa. The app uses artificial intelligence to provide personalised, location-based guided tours – offering historical context, cultural insights, and practical travel information at your fingertips.

Whether you are exploring a city, a national park, or a heritage site, the app learns your interests and adapts the experience in real time. It's like having a knowledgeable local guide in your pocket, available offline and in multiple languages.

This project represents our commitment to building technology that showcases Africa's beauty, history, and innovation.

Why It Matters

A Local Guide
In Your Pocket

The African AI Tour Guide is built for both international visitors and local explorers who want a richer, more personal experience of the continent's incredible heritage.

Key Features

  • AI-personalised tours based on your interests
  • Location-based historical & cultural context
  • Available fully offline – no signal required
  • Multi-language support across African languages
  • Compatible with iOS (App Store) & Android (Google Play)
  • Real-time adaptive tour experiences
